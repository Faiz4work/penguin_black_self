FAQ

General

What is badmintontv.com?

badmintontv.com consists of a small group of passionate badminton fans who want to improve the landscape of badminton highlight videos. We weren't satisfied with the current landscape of the badminton highlights scene, and therefore wanted to improve it by creating our own highlights it with the badminton community, in hopes that everyone can enjoy the videos, just like we do.

What can I watch on badmintontv.com?

Currently, we have multiple highlight videos of all tournaments (with a few exclusions, listed below) uploaded to the official BWF YouTube channel, dating back to the beginning of 2018.

What is the benefits of subscription?

With subscription, you can watch the complete archive on our site, where each match consists of a "Highlights" and "Extended Highlights" video. Details of the subscription fee can be found here .

Why is there no free subscription tier?

Due to the size of our servers, and our limited funding, it's simply impossible to offer a limited, free experience of the site, as it's a real possibility of the site crashing due to too much demand. We aim to list our subscription fees as afforable as possible, so that many people have the option of joining.

What is the difference between "Highlights" and "Extended Highlights"?

"Highlights" contains the best rallies of the match, while "Extended Highlights" is a compilation of all rallies, point-by-point.

How long after a match will the highlights be available

We aim to upload highlight videos within 24 hours after the live match finishes. However, the timing also depends on the location and time zone of the tournament, as our AI is not 100% automated, and we are only a small team of people.

Which tournaments are excluded?

The following tournaments are excluded from the site, for various reasons:
  • Olympics
  • Asian Games
  • European Games
  • Commonwealth Games
  • SEA Games
  • A handful of lower-ranking BWF tournaments

    • Why does the archive only begin in 2018?

    Due to current technology limitations, our AI can't process lower-quality videos. Starting 2018, most matches were high enough quality for it to process, hence why we decided to start the archive there.

    How do I search for matches?

    Our site does not have a dedicated search bar. Instead, you can find the matches using the navigation bar to filter based on tournament, year, player and country. This feature may be added in the future.

    Can I watch on a phone, tablet and computer?

    badmintontv.com is accessible on almost every modern browser, and on almost every modern device with an internet connection. If you have a reasonably recent phone, tablet or computer, you should be fine.

    You will need a solid and stable broadband internet connection in order to watch videos. There are a few sample videos on the home page for you to watch and see if your device and internet connection meet our requirement.

    If your internet connection is not fast enough, you can try lowering the resolution of the video.
